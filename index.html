About Us

Founded by Ishinder Singh, a computer science student at Chandigarh University, IRA transforms advanced research into practical solutions through cutting-edge technology.

Our mission is to bridge theoretical research and real-world applications, focusing on automation, AI, and sustainable technology innovations.
