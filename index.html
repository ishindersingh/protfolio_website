About Us

Founded by Ishinder Singh, a passionate computer science student at Chandigarh University, IRA was born from a vision to transform cutting-edge research into real-world solutions.

At IRA, we leverage deep technology to drive innovation and create sustainable impact. With a strong foundation in computer science and an entrepreneurial mindset, our founder leads the way in technological advancement.

Our mission is simple yet ambitious: to bridge the gap between research and reality, turning ideas into inventions and prototypes into market-ready products. We are committed to pushing the boundaries of automation, artificial intelligence, and sustainable technology.

Our Vision

IRA is dedicated to transforming advanced research into real-world solutions by bridging the gap between education, innovation, and technology. We aim to create a global knowledge hub that offers cutting-edge technical education, empowering aspiring innovators through specialized courses, hands-on training, and industry-driven programs.

By reinvesting revenue from education into research and development, we ensure a sustainable cycle of innovation, turning ideas into inventions and learners into leaders.
